Mailing List

The UMBC Linux Users' Group runs a mailing list for community discussion, requests for help and suggestions, announcements, and information about upcoming events. To interact with and receive messages from our list, you must subscribe to it. If you would like to see past messages, our archives are public.

To Subscribe

Visit our mailing list page or send an email to sympa@lists.umbc.edu containing only the body

sub umbclinux

To change the amount of email you receive from this list, or otherwise change your subscription options, visit the subscription options page. To send email and participate in our list, send email to umbclinux@lists.umbc.edu, or use list-reply from your email client to respond to a particular message.

Or, to unsubscribe, send a message to sympa@lists.umbc.edu containing only the body

unsubscribe umbclinux
