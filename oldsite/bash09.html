UMBC Linux User’s Group 12th Anniversary /bin/bash Party

Held Sunday, October 25, 2009 at Sorrento of Arbutus.

The Linux User’s Group held an enjoyable anniversary /bin/bash (party) on October 25, 2009. Members both on-campus and off- had the chance to meet past UMBC graduates and members from the mailing list in person. A great time was had by all.
